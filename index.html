The Problem

Informal Finance is Broken

"People trust their neighbors more than banks, but neighbors are human and systems are manual."

  • • Fragility: One default collapses the entire savings group.
  • • Opacity: No system of record creates disputes over who paid.
  • • Invisibility: Users get no credit for years of perfect performance.
The Solution

The Coordination Layer

"We don't replace the Ajo; we give it a digital exoskeleton."

  • • Automation: Rule-based reminders and payout orchestration.
  • • Transparency: Immutable ledger of all contributions.
  • • Zero Risk: We never touch user funds; we sit on top of bank rails.
