The Problem

Informal savings (Ajo/Esusu) are culturally massive but functionally fragile. In Nigeria alone, tens of millions participate, yet the system relies on manual labor and high-stakes social trust.

  • ✕ Trust Breaks at Scale: One default collapses the chain.
  • ✕ Manual Burden: Relies on tedious collection and meetings.
  • ✕ Lack of Records: Disputes over payments and history.

Our Solution

A Coordination Layer for Group Savings

We don't replace Ajo; we digitize it. We provide the visibility and enforcement while third-party payment rails handle the movement of money.

Group-defined rules & schedules
Automated reminders & orchestration
Transparent contribution/payout logs
